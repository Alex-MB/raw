K1815

The K1815, KM1815 series, designed to build specialized devices for parallel-pipeline (systolic) digital signal processing systems,
series include the following types:
K1815VF1 - universal 8-bit element (weight summation circuit);
K1815VF2 - linear build-up circuit (accumulative adder);
K1815VF3 - microprocessor for fast Fourier transform (signal processor);
K1815IA1 - arithmetic logic unit;
K1815IM1 - eight-input adder (8x2) of consecutive numbers;
K1815IR1 - scheme of magazine-type shift registers (orthogonal matrix of shift registers);
K1815PR1 - converter of codes of consecutive numbers.
LSIs of the K1815 set are intended for designing high-performance digital signal processing systems,
using the principles of parallelization of the processing process and pipelining of calculations in each
parallel channel and are implemented on the basis of I2L-TTL (K1815VF1, K1815VF2), I2L (K1815VFZ) and Schotki-TTL (K1815IM1, K1815PR1, K1815IR1, K1815IA1).
LSIs K1815VF1, K1815IM1, K1815IR1, K1815PR1 provide operations related to the calculation of sums of products of real numbers,
as well as operations with complex data, digital filtering operations using additional external elements
to organize the process control of calculation and synchronization of data streams.
